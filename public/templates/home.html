About Us


New Old Road is an in-house team of experienced designers, web developers and marketers based in Los Angeles, California and Tel Aviv, Israel. While we enjoy making websites and web apps of all kind, we specialize in native mobile on-demand application development for Apple iOS through the App Store and Android through the Google Play Store.

On-demand apps are often characterized as the “Uber or Airbnb for ‘blank’.” This means that most of the products we build use payment systems. These are the types of apps that users need and actually desire to engage with in order to obtain a service by entering their credit cards. This takes the question of “how will we monetize?” out of the equation.

We are also open to hearing about your idea for a social application and have selectively chosen to work on such products, but one of our main concerns is ensuring that our clients will be able to make a considerable return on their investment. On-demand apps fill a specific need for the consumer and make life easier.

At New Old Road, we are passionate about design; specifically User Interface and User Experience (UI/UX) Design. While functionality is obviously our top priority, we also know that users are well trained to judge their experience within the first few seconds of interacting with the platform. The myth that one can build a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) and that users will continue to use the product after they have downloaded it has all but been debunked. This is because users are savvy and expect the desktop and mobile products they use to not only work perfectly, but also look great and be fun to use. The idea that functionality comes first and design can come later can be likened to someone starting a car company with a Ferrari engine, but the body of a pinto. The truth of the matter is that functionality and UI/UX Design go hand in hand. New Old Road is committed to building apps that people will not only download, but will want to continue to interact with in the long term.
